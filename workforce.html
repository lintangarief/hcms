WORKFORCE PLANNING

A strategic Workforce Planning is a whole level of framework that compare internal and external demand-supply data to identify workforce and also our talent gaps within the organization. This strategic planning enable Telkom to improve business planning, decision making and measure all outcomes.

Here's the Key Challenges to deliver the strategic business outcomes for the Group resolved

Create a standard definition of "workforce" to establish a common understanding. The definition of workforce (organic/ inorganic) differs across CFUs/ FUs.

The HC Analytics tool has to bridge the gap between workforce data availibility and consistency varies across group companies.

Since the FUs and subsidiaries are functioning in a matrix structure, it's challenging to estimare the workforce aligne with teh CFU's.

The existence of inconsistencies in the job families/ jobs within the goup companies creates challenges in data collection & integration

The workforce mapping is not completed according to job families and CFUs/ FUs during the organization structure evolving. This is impacting to historical data collection and causing difficulty in establishing pas workforce trends to identify root causes in workforce planning errors.

Dynaplan tool has not been extended to CFUs/ FUs and thus data is not streamlined, causing difficulty to collect and interpret.

The existing model is enable to link the business objectives with workforce plan e.g. productivity increase. Therefore the tool does not show a realistic workforce plan as the business driver growth

The existing tool does not incorporate detailed workload analysis into workforce plan.

To overcome those Key Challenges, here is a new approach for the way foward

Today
  • Top-down approach
  • data gathered at Corporate Level
  • Drivers not diffentiated for different business
Tomorrow
  • Implement Governance Model for Workforce Planning
  • Streamline data entry for Workforce Types
  • Update Workforce Planning Process
  • Tools to identify workforce gaps:
  • 1. WFP Macro Model (Excel-based) aims to accomodate the appropriate equilibrium between the bottom-up and the top-down approach. It is designed to provide a more solid foundation of FTE distribution by considering the Demand Driver included as prioritization parameters
  • 2. PoC Tool (Tableau-based) has the capabilities to showcase supply-demand gap for the Telkom regional function, drilling down by job familiy, band positions and job prefix. The gap is derived based on an optimization function, aimed at keeping personel cost minimal while meeting revenue and EBITDA increments

Maintenance of quality data (of at least 5 years) is critical to enable any predictive analytics required in the future. One way we can kick start the streamlining of data is to align on the types of workforce we have and how we input data for them (1/3)
