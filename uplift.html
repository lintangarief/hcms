Uplift Human Capital Capability

Once again, It will be always more challenges for the organization like us in finding the way to survive even fit in the future. How do we ensure our capabilities would drive the Group in the right way? the People, Culture, and Organization are the answer for this transformation. The past emerging talent environment has to adjust as the customer-centric approaches will be a priority and agility is a necessity. So, here's HCM plays it role make it work.

4 Clicks Only

WHAT IF WE - Enable employees to access HR policies in a matter of 4 click or even less. An overwhelming majority of HCM employees are caught up with transactional and manual work such as responding to general HC-related matters, most of which can actually be addressed by an effective employee self-service portal

HOW WE COULD START WHAT WE EXPECT TO LEARN
  • Perform a requirements analysis to inform the Self-Service functionalities required and partner selection by answering the questions such as what the self-service needs to do, how secure does the data need to be
  • Determine key policies to be included in the prototype, engaging sample customers to obtain their input as relevant
  • Engage partner(s) to design interface and build the prototype
  • Validate prototype design with sample customers to test for usability
  • We would empower customers to take on the responsibility to find information to address their HC-related queries themselves
  • We would learn user-centered design methodology, with a focus on the technology
  • We would understand customer priorities and preferences from portal usage trends
  • We would improve HCM effectiveness and efficiency by minimizing time to process requests that could be handled much more effectively through automation

Let it Go

WHAT IF WE - Take the transactional workload off the HC Business Partners (HCBPs). Today they spend too much time on supporting the business on administrative HC activities such as conducting routine case work or chasing up managers for performance review documentation, leaving little time for the value-adding strategic work their roles were originally launched for. What if we free them up from admin work and help them thrive?

HOW WE COULD START WHAT WE EXPECT TO LEARN
  • Conduct current state assessment through the means of focus groups or job shadowing to capture the as-is role of HCBPs, and map against their as-is Job Description and/or role profile, or conduct a detailed activity analysis survey
  • Develop as-is and to-be personas of HCBPs from the captured pain points and opportunities
  • Pinpoint specific activities and tasks currently performed by HCBPs to be moved to Operational Services or automated through the means of Self-Service Portal, or simply eliminated
  • We would learn how to redesign and optimize the business partner role to focus on strategic HC work

  • We would re-align HCM to strategic platforms to address high-impact needs of the business
  • We would understand priority features that the Self-Service portal must accommodate to enable seamless service delivery
