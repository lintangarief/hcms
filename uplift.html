Uplift Human Capital Capability

Once again, It will be always more challenges for the organization like us in finding the way to survive even fit in the future. How do we ensure our capabilities would drive the Group in the right way? the People, Culture, and Organization are the answer for this transformation. The past emerging talent environment has to adjust as the customer-centric approaches will be a priority and agility is a necessity. So, here's HCM plays it role make it work.

What are we solving for?

"HC Development VP is main go-to person on all People & culture matters"

What are we solving for?

“HCM has little visibility on how their operations and programs are working in the subsidiaries”

What are we solving for?

“HC are implementing many strategic initiatives but the employees are not fully aware and have little involvement”

What are we solving for?

“There are multiple data sources and the recent organization transformation from product to customer-facing units have complicated data management”

What are we solving for?

“Different CFUs collect and use data differently as there is no guideline and governance”

What are we solving for?

“There are many HC tools with similar functionalities across the group, some with low adoption rate”

What are we solving for?

“There are no predictive capabilities in the current workforce planning tool that can give us a staffing plan for the entire group”

What are we solving for?

“Telkom needs to grow aggressively but we are still in the exploration stage of using advanced technology that could improve their productivity”

Program’s Objectives and Deliverables:

Adjust the role of HCM to adjust to an emerging talent environment that is dramatically different from the past with a focus on customer-centricity.

• HC Operating Model Design • Establishing HC CoEs/ Empower Community • HC Operations of the Future • Increase HC Capability • Boost HC Credibility • Enhance Workforce Planning • HC Datafication and Analytics • Integrated HCIS • Future of Workforce Planning (WFP) • Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Robotics • Roadmap to Commercialize CorpU and ACI •

WHAT IF WE - Encourage employees to implement Digital Working Style by rewarding those who are adapting certain Digital Working Style behavior through provision of attractive incentives (i.e. point systems, recognition/award, financial reward)

HOW WE COULD START WHAT WE EXPECT TO LEARN
  • Identify the Digital Working Style behavior that will set as the foundation for the reward system
  • Assess the current reward system and its effectiveness to drive changes (if any)
  • Conduct Lab approach to identify and understand the best reward system derived from customer experience
  • Design the point system and approach based on Lab output
  • We would focus on customer centricity to gain attention as well as promoting the system to employee
  • We would have higher customer engagement and higher adaption to the program
