FLEXIBLE & AGILE ORGANIZATION FOR
ORGANIC AND INORGANIC GROWTH

In the new transformation, Telkom Group has to step out of the traditional way, from telecommunication business into digitized enterprises, and also a build-up of the digital services business. So, how the Group the Group could compete and adapt in a complex environment and uncertain future? Let's unlock a flexible, team-based, and networked organization to reach our ambition!

What are we solving for?

“We have no single source to help us look for the knowledge and skills that exist within the Group”

What are we solving for?

“We need to be better at cross-functional collaboration. Career paths and reward structures are not flexible and do not support cross-functional collaboration for us to fully gain synergy”

What are we solving for?

““We lack the skills to aggressively follow our ambition to grow inorganically” ”

Program’s Objectives and Deliverables:

Unlock a flexible, team-based, and networked organization for the Group that is capable of competing effectively in a complex environment and adapting quickly to an uncertain future

• Telkom People Network • Telkom Open Talent • Setup and Test the Flexible Organization • Flexible Career Paths and Rewards • Simplify Performance Management for Flexible Organization • Establishing M&A Advisory Unit •

Experiment
KNOW YOUR EMPLOYEES

WHAT IF WE - start designing the online profile for Telkom People Network by identifying the “skills” that managers would search for when looking for people to join their teams

HOW WE COULD START WHAT WE EXPECT TO LEARN
  • Start asking hiring managers what skills they look for in potential candidates
  • Review the skill profiles created with employees if they are able to relate to identified skill sets and/or suggest additional skills they feel are relevant
  • We would have an understanding on the desired skills of employees today, in terms of capabilities and competencies
  • We would get input for design of the appropriate tagging/ list of skills to be chosen in the Telkom People Network platform
POTENTIAL ITERATIONS
  • We could develop the menu of competencies which employees can tag themselves to for the Telkom People
  • Network based on the input from this experiment
EXPECTED OUTPUT (not limited to)
Online profile prototype/Template for list of skills
