TRENDS & CHALLENGES

"Keep your eyes on the stars, and your feet on the ground"
- Teddy Roosevelt -

The Global Telco is now and future. That trends are undoubtedly affecting TIMES market

Network Security

Telco operators are expected to play a pivotal role in deploying cybersecurity solutions in a holistic manner. Therefore, Telkom need to identify the key competencies to fit with new job roles in network security specialist and source.

5G NETWORK

Asian operator are expected to collaborate and develop the infrastructure to enable 5G services by 2020. Telkom require manpower to bring transpormative 5G services to the customers

DIGITAL ECOSYSTEMS

Many telco operators are partnering with IT solution providers and transforming to become ICT companies. Telkom should adjust policy and process framework to speed up and enable organic and inorganic growth plans

DATA AND ANALYTICS

47% of operators had big data investments in place and 19% are expect to implement big data strategy at some point in 2015. So, Telkom need to identify and build talents with analytical capabilities to crunch big data, in order to grasp the customers

MACHINE LEARNING

Over 300 million smartphones will have on-board neural network machine learning capability. Telkom has to use machine learning to develop customer service app for internally and externally

Furthermore, there’s also Human Capital trends which altogether they are aimed at paving the way to Global Fortune 500 Aspiration!

ORGANIZATION OF THE FUTURE

Because the future is digital, we need to move faster, quickly adapt, learn fast, and embrace the dynamic career demands. Telkom need to design and operate with A (legacy) and B (digital) model in mind: relook at the policies, processes and structure to accommodate for disruption at the edges.

PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

Companies have radically changed the way they measure, evaluate, and recognize employee performance. So, Telkom's management should simplify the process and policy to aim agile their goal, check-ins and continuous feedback with new models of evaluation and rewards

EMPLOYEE EXPERIENCE

Leading organizations are broadening their focus from culture to the entire employee experience, from the first contact through retirement and beyond. Telkom need understand the HC customer personas, their journey and milestones to improve the service delivery and increase employee engagement

TALENT ACQUISITION

Recruiting is becoming a digital experience as recruiters use new technology to forge connections with candidates and strengthen the employment brand. So, to increase recruitment efficiency, Telkom required gaining ability to leverage emerging tools and analytics that are shifting from push to pull

CAREERS AND LEARNING

Companies are driving toward "always-on" learning experiences that allow employees to build skills quickly on their own terms. Therefor, to enable real-time, on-demand learning, Telkom has to customize learning experience of content and mediums through drawing on expertise from ecosystem and employing new technologies
