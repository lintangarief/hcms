OPTIMIZE TALENT AND LEADERSHIP FOR DIGITAL FUTURE

In digital era HCM has to play an exciting practice to attract, develop, and retain the best-in-class talents as the leaders and employees demand at it. The market also desire the same from the Telkom Group as the leading digital native organization to provide the most seamless and tailored experience. Now, it becomes a challenge to give an equivalent measurement between the employee and the Group as an employer in digital industry dynamics. If it's done right, you ought to feel the impact directly.

What are we solving for?

“We recruit new graduates twice a year and have a general development program. It takes time to know if they fit their assigned job (sales) and will be rotated to support function later if they don’t”

What are we solving for?

“There is no motivation for mobility as there is no benefit or good positions for the employees when they return”

What are we solving for?

“Our Leaders need to support behaviours and policies that enable Telkom to “Be Digital”

FIT FOR PURPOSE RECRUITMENT

Telkom Group will facing a scale of retirement of talents. Therefore, the need to redesign recruitment of fresh competent become significant to sustain and grow the organization. The approach has to be attractive for bi-annual Fresh Graduate hiring and ad-hoc experienced hiring for all employee cohorts across the Group. Make sure it retains the key employees demands by shorten the multi-pronged recruitment strategy.
