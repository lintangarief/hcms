Culture – Infusing Digital and
Innovation into The Telkom Way

No doubt that today to the future ahead, digital technology is an essential part of any modern company and impacting their working and workers culture. To thrive in the changing times, the organization must be allowing quick adjustment to have productive and positive culture that embody connectivity, engagement, and flexibility. So, are you ready to embrace a digital strategy as part our whole professional life?

Designing Reward for Implementing Digital

WHAT IF WE - Encourage employees to implement Digital Working Style by rewarding those who are adapting certain Digital Working Style behavior through provision of attractive incentives (i.e. point systems, recognition/award, financial reward)

HOW WE COULD START WHAT WE EXPECT TO LEARN
  • Identify the Digital Working Style behavior that will set as the foundation for the reward system
  • Assess the current reward system and its effectiveness to drive changes (if any)
  • Conduct Lab approach to identify and understand the best reward system derived from customer experience
  • Design the point system and approach based on Lab output
  • We would focus on customer centricity to gain attention as well as promoting the system to employee
  • We would have higher customer engagement and higher adaption to the program

Utilizing the Open Office Space Effectively

WHAT IF WE - Encourage people to embrace the open office culture. So, what if we try to test if current facilities are being used effectively (e.g. treadmill, open spaces) and provide feedback for design of future offices/ improve current use of space

HOW WE COULD START WHAT WE EXPECT TO LEARN
  • Analyze the current situation through survey and observation
  • Promote and encourage employees to maximize the use of Open Office Space
  • Observing the trends and changes for specific period of time (e.g. 2 weeks)
  • Monitor the result to provide feedback / recommendation for future design
  • We would understand people objectives, behavior and trends in using Open Office Space
  • We would measure the link between happiness and its relation to business productivity and engagement

Culture change definitely will impact the culture of organization. In order to measure a true reflection of the situation in the Group, we find the best-fit mechanism to track, measure, and improve cultural programs. It should focus on realization of desired behaviors to establish efficacy of the transformation.

HOW WE COULD START WHAT WE EXPECT TO LEARN

  • Communicate and socialize the ‘Digital Trait’ that is going to be measured

  • We would know which behavior is the most and least being exhibited by the employee
