Culture – Infusing Digital and
Innovation into The Telkom Way

No doubt that today to the future ahead, digital technology is an essential part of any modern company and impacting their working and workers culture. To thrive in the changing times, the organization must be allowing quick adjustment to have productive and positive culture that embody connectivity, engagement, and flexibility. So, are you ready to embrace a digital strategy as part our whole professional life?

What are we solving for?

“The challenge is to promote and influence the Telkom way to the subsidiaries and how to make digital culture happen”

What are we solving for?

“How do we foster a culture of innovation in all areas of the business?”

What are we solving for?

“We have ongoing cultural programs but we are not sure if we have a robust way to measure the effectiveness apart from the yearly Entropy survey”

Program’s Objectives and Deliverables:

Drive a productive and positive culture associated with connectivity, engagement, and flexibility across the Group in line with efforts to fully embrace digital strategy.

• Agile WOW (Change Enablement) • Activate Digital Innovation • Culture M&M (Monitor and Measure) •

What ideas and actions can we initiate?

AGILE WOW (CHANGE ENABLEMENT)

In the digital era, Telkom needs implementing mechanism that involves the employees, in order to focus on targeted personas and desire behaviors. This change could accomodate the the new agile way of work.
